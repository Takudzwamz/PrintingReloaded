FAQ's

What materials do we use?
All of our products are made with PLA Plastic Filament. PLA Is the most commonly used 3d printing material. it is bio-degradable and strong enough to be used for a lot of applications including home products, spare parts etc.
How do I order?
You can order online here on our website, or contact us on our provided contact details.
How long will my order take to process?
3D printing is a slow process with some prints taking a few minutes to completre and some several days. How long an order takes depends entirely on how big the order is.

Unfortunately we are not a walk in store yet, but you can visit our factory/studio at the provided address below.
Can I order over the phone or email?
